How Does Chombox Accelerate Impact?

We deliver franchise-based solutions that equip emerging market entrepreneurs and missional organizations to launch and manage sustainable businesses. Our solutions focus on health, learning, retail and living.

Health Solutions

Chombox Health solutions are small-footprint, franchise-based facilities that accelerate access to quality and affordable medical care for low and middle income populations.

Our facilities are purpose-built to match a community's needs and can include outpatient primary care clinics, small in-patient hospitals, maternity birthing clinics and stand-alone diagnostic, pharmacy or dental kiosks.
